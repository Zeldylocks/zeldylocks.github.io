Mobile Drag and Drop

Crazy Virus

Testing out a reaction based drag and drop game with touch controls for the mobile devices, where the player has to keep the viruses apart from each other for a predetermined time. Viruses move around randomly, bouncing off the walls.
Scripts: DragAndDrop, GameMaster, MenuPatrol, RandomPatrol.

Endless Runner

Legend of Bootleg

Brushing up on how we made the Moonwalker, this endless runner provides a five-point lane movement which the player moves and where the enemies spawn. Blue enemies shoot projectiles. The player can get hit total of 2 times, and the third hit ends the game, prompting the end screen.
Scripts: Destroyer, Health, Obstacle, Player, RepeatingBG, Restart, ScoreManager, Spawner, and SpawnPoint.

Naming conventions. Every time I managed to get a scrip working, I recorded a clip and sent it to my friend via discord (hense the clideo time stamp on the clip, since I had to compress it for discord). First one was called "Legend of Bootleg". But the second video required a new name to avoid version confusion, so I started to follow the zelda naming conventions, and the next clip was called "Bootleg's Awakening", and "Bootleg to the Past" followed.
