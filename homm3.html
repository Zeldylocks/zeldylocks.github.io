Zelda Inspired Levels

Amongst a few other early PC games, Heroes 3 was one of my first segways to the game design and content creation world. It came with a map editor that allowed players to make their own custom maps and campaigns. Unfortunately, this happened back around the year 2000-2001, so only a few selected levels have survived the journey to this day. I have the original map files, but those are not compatible with the HD remake the company released on steam a few years ago. Luckily anyone with the original game can place these map files into their map folder and play the the levels. Contact me if you want those levels.

I made multiple maps back then, but they often followed a similar pattern; make a zelda game. I was, and still am, a huge zelda fan, and the old zelda formula will probably never leave me. Below are three levels that I made, all of which include a subterranean map world. Two of the levels use this parallel map as a space where the dungeons are, but because Ocarina of Time had a parallel adventure, the dungeons in this level are replaced with plain boss fights. Additionally, since the audio files of the game reside in a dedicated folder, I had provided a separate audio folder for my friends so that they could replace the original game audio files with zelda audio!

The three levels that survived are Early Travels, Ocarina of Time, and the Wind Waker levels, as presented below from left to right.
